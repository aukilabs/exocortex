**Signal restructuring**

**Memetic propagation**

## Routines

What are the recurring activities you perform to fulfill your responsibilities? Be specific — include cadences, times, and what good execution looks like.

**Scan sources**

Periodically scan my sources to see if there is any new information.

**Create a digest**

I take the collected signals and repackage them according to my list of recipients, taking my recipients' interests and preferences into account.

**Publish and nudge**

I send the digest to my list of recipients, and ask them if they consumed it.
